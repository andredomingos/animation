A quick and simple switch
All the participating banks are part of the Current Account Switch Service - this is a free service that makes switching banks quick and simple. If you choose to switch, in most cases, your new bank will process everything for you through this service, transferring your balance, Direct Debits and standing orders to your new account, and closing your old account when the switch is complete.
Help with switching
If you have any questions about switching, visit the Support page, where you'll find lots more information on the scheme and how it works.
Our Business Banking Switch team is also on hand to explain the switching process, step by step. You can call them on 0800 151 2991, Minicom 0800 092 9098, or +44 161 933 8119 from abroad.
